About Me

In case you were wondering... It's pronounced "Tie-Lie" and it originates from an old Chinese proverb.

I'm a 1A Computer Science and Business Administration Double Degree student at the University of Waterloo. I have a burning passion for technology and a desire to empower and impact my communities.

In my spare time, I love rock climbing, hiking, and the outdoors. I regularly attend and organize hackathons to meet inspiring people and develop cool stuff. I'm also a community leader with heavy involvement in different initiatives centered around education and youth athletics.

I'm currently a Co-founder & Developer at RunFar Studios, and a Frontend Developer at GoldenHack. I'm searching for Software/Data internships for the Spring 2020 season!
